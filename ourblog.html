    About Us

    We are GiftDesk

    Giftdesk, a wedding and corporate event management company located in Amravati, India, produce a spectrum of events in wedding events, award celebrations, gala dinners and conferences of distinct volumes. We offer a bespoke planning service, make arrangements, create games that suit your requirements and tastes, such as planning, messaging, etc. to make sure that each event exhibits your aspirations.

    Our Vision

    We started our journey with local events and here we are today, doing destination wedding events, corporate events, and other special occasions. Our services include weddings, birthday parties, trade shows, weddings, award functions, fashion shows, school and college fests, etc.

    Core Goal

    To respect the singularity of each event, the cultural difference of attendees to every single event.

    To follow a “client comes first” approach and content before money.

    To offer timeliness, financial responsibility, management efficiency and highest ethical standards.


    Objective

    We are here to let you enjoy your special day with your family and guests, while we walk that extra mile for you and offers a "One Stop Destination" for all your event management requirements.
